Data Architecture & Telemetry

Retro Console Pro values your data autonomy. This application operates entirely on a client-side architecture.

  • Local State Management: Your preferences, high scores, and application configurations are processed and stored locally within your browser environment.
  • No Cloud Infrastructure: We do not deploy backend cloud tracking servers. No diagnostic analytics, gameplay telemetry, or personal tracking metrics are intercepted or collected.
  • Zero Cookies: This application runtime operates cleanly without the injection of tracking cookies or third-party analytical code elements.
